# Stackfolio — Support

Need help with the app? We're happy to help.

**Email:** [ibrahim.zakarya93@gmail.com](mailto:ibrahim.zakarya93@gmail.com)

We typically respond within 2 business days.

## Common topics

- **Restoring purchases** — Settings → Restore Purchases.
- **iCloud sync (Premium)** — Settings → enable iCloud Sync. Requires app restart and an active iCloud account.
- **Prices look stale** — Pull down on the Settings screen to force a refresh, or relaunch the app.
- **Cancel subscription** — iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Stackfolio.

For anything else, email us at the address above and include your iOS version and what you were doing when the issue happened.
